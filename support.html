Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I upload a file or image?

To upload a file or image, open the app and tap on "Upload a File or Image" on the main screen. You can select PDFs, text files, or images from your device.

2. How do I generate a summary?

After uploading your file, navigate to the "Summary" tab and tap on "Generate Summary." The app will process your content and provide a concise summary tailored to your educational level.

3. How do I create and take quizzes?

Go to the "Quiz" tab, select the number of questions you want, and tap on "Generate Quiz." Once the quiz is ready, you can answer the questions and submit to see your results.

4. Is my data secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and data security. Please read our Privacy Policy for more details.
